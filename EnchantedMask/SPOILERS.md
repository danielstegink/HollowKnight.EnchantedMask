# Glyphs

This file contains a list of all glyphs, their statistics, and how to unlock them.

## Glyph of Exploration
![Explorer](https://github.com/danielstegink/HollowKnight.EnchantedMask/blob/main/EnchantedMask/Resources/Glyphs/Explorer.png?raw=true)
 - Tier: Common
 - Unlocked by purchasing all of Cornifer's maps
 - Increases movement speed

## Glyph of Childhood
![Grub](https://github.com/danielstegink/HollowKnight.EnchantedMask/blob/main/EnchantedMask/Resources/Glyphs/Grub.png?raw=true)
 - Tier: Rare
 - Unlocked by rescuing all the grubs and receive Grufather's final reward
 - Increases SOUL gained from Grubsong and damage dealt by Grubberfly's Elegy

## Glyph of Deceit
![False](https://github.com/danielstegink/HollowKnight.EnchantedMask/blob/main/EnchantedMask/Resources/Glyphs/False.png?raw=true)
 - Tier: Common
 - Unlocked by defeating the False Knight
 - Reduces Stagger requirement of bosses

## Glyph of Shamanism
![Snail](https://github.com/danielstegink/HollowKnight.EnchantedMask/blob/main/EnchantedMask/Resources/Glyphs/Snail.png?raw=true)
 - Tier: Uncommon
 - Unlocked by fully upgrading all spells and unlocking Soul Eater
 - Increases passive SOUL regeneration

## Glyph of the Hunter
![Hunter](https://github.com/danielstegink/HollowKnight.EnchantedMask/blob/main/EnchantedMask/Resources/Glyphs/Hunter.png?raw=true)
 - Tier: Rare
 - Unlocked by earning the Hunter's Mark
 - Increases all damage dealt

## Glyph of the Past
![Dash](https://github.com/danielstegink/HollowKnight.EnchantedMask/blob/main/EnchantedMask/Resources/Glyphs/Dash.png?raw=true)
